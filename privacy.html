Privacy Policy for AmraX

Last Updated: December 2025

Introduction

AmraX, developed by Abdelali El Amrani (Amrania Labs), respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AmraX application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your email address and authentication credentials
  • Profile Data: Any information you voluntarily enter into your tasks, goals, or execution logs
  • Communication Data: If you contact us for support, we collect your messages and attachments

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

AmraX uses Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics. These services automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, execution sessions completed, performance metrics
  • Analytics Data: Session duration, timestamps, interaction patterns
  • Crash Reports: Technical errors to improve app stability

Important: No personally identifying data such as your full name, email address, phone number, or payment information is collected through these analytics services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve App functionality
  • Track your execution performance and analytics
  • Display relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve stability
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enhance user experience and app features

3. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

  • Displays personalized and non-personalized advertisements
  • Collects anonymous usage and device data for ad relevance
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase Analytics

  • Tracks app usage patterns and feature adoption
  • Collects crash and performance data
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Play Services

  • Handles app authentication and installation
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Secure data transmission via HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication tokens for account protection
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Compliance with Google Play security guidelines

However, no transmission method over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. User Control & Rights

5.1 Ad Personalization

You can opt out of personalized ads:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Google → Manage Your Google Account
  3. Select Data & Privacy
  4. Under "Ad settings," toggle off "Personalized Ads"

5.2 Data Access & Deletion

You can:

  • Export your execution data from the App settings
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Contact us at support@amrania.com for data requests

6. Data Retention

  • Execution Data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • Analytics Data: Automatically deleted after 12 months by Firebase
  • Ad Data: Managed by Google AdMob per their privacy policy

7. Children's Privacy

AmraX is not designed for users under age 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the App. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
